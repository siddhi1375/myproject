⛰️ Mountain Packing Checklist

1 Day Hike

  • 🥾 Hiking Boots
    Durable, waterproof boots with excellent ankle support for rough mountain trails. Ensure good traction.
  • 🎒 Daypack / Rucksack
    Carry essentials like water, snacks, GPS, and first-aid kit.
  • 💧 Hydration Pack / Water Bottle
    Stay hydrated during intense hiking. Minimum 2L recommended.
  • 🧭 Navigation Tools
    Map, compass, or GPS device to safely navigate trails.
  • 📸 GoPro / Camera
    Capture adventure moments. Extra batteries recommended.
  • 👕 Clothing
    1 set of quick-dry clothing and socks suitable for 1-day hike.
  • 🛠️ First Aid / Emergency Kit
    Bandages, antiseptic wipes, painkillers, whistle, and small flashlight.

For 2 Days

  • ✅ Includes all items from 1 Day
  • 🎒 Backpack / Trekking Rucksack
    Comfortable for multi-day hiking with layered clothing, food, and camping essentials.
  • 👕 Clothing
    2 sets of clothes, 2 pairs of socks, and innerwear to rotate over 2 days.
  • 🛏️ Tent & Sleeping Bag
    Lightweight waterproof tent and warm sleeping bag for overnight stay.
  • 🍴 Portable Cooking Kit
    Stove, fuel, utensils, and lightweight cookware for 2-day meals.
  • 🛠️ Multi-tool / Survival Kit
    Knife, fire starter, whistle, emergency blanket, and first-aid essentials.

More Than 2 Days

  • ✅ Includes all items from 1 & 2 Days
  • 👕 Clothing
    3+ sets of clothing with multiple pairs of socks and innerwear for extended trekking.
  • 🎒 Large Backpack / Trekking Rucksack
    Carry clothing, food, cooking gear, and camping essentials for long trips.
  • 🛏️ Tent & Sleeping Bag
    Waterproof tent and warm sleeping bag for multiple nights.
  • 🍴 Portable Cooking Kit
    Stove, fuel, utensils, and high-energy meals for extended stays.
  • 📸 GoPro / Camera
    Extra batteries and memory cards for long trips.
  • 🛠️ Multi-tool / Survival Kit
    Emergency essentials including first-aid, fire starter, and whistle.
