🏞️ Lake & River Packing Checklist

For 1 Day

  • 💧 Reusable Water Bottle (2L)
    Stay hydrated with at least 2 liters of water.
  • 🍫 Snacks & Energy Bars
    Light, high-energy food to maintain stamina.
  • 📸 GoPro Camera
    Capture water adventures; use waterproof housing.
  • 👕 Comfortable Clothing
    Quick-dry set suitable for outdoor & water activities.
  • 👟 Water Shoes
    Protect feet while walking in lakes or rivers.
  • 🛠️ First Aid / Emergency Kit
    Bandages, antiseptic wipes, painkillers, whistle, small flashlight.

For 2 Days

  • ✅ Includes all items from 1 Day
  • ⛺ Portable Tent
    Lightweight & waterproof for resting near the lake/river.
  • 👕 Extra Clothes (3 Sets)
    Rotate daily to stay dry & comfortable.
  • 🩹 First Aid Kit
    Bandages, antiseptic, medicines, and essentials for 2 days.
  • 📸 GoPro Camera
    Bring extra batteries & memory cards for longer trips.

For More Than 2 Days

  • ✅ Includes all items from 1 & 2 Days
  • 🍳 Cooking Kit
    Portable stove, utensils, ready-to-cook meals.
  • 🛏️ Sleeping Bag
    Warm & waterproof for colder nights.
  • 🔋 Power Bank / Solar Charger
    Keep devices charged during longer trips.
  • 🛠️ First Aid / Emergency Kit
    Comprehensive kit including bandages, antiseptic, painkillers, whistle, flashlight.

🍲 If You Plan to Cook Near Lake

  • 🔥 Portable Grill / Camping Stove
    Compact and safe for lakeside cooking.
  • 🍴 Utensils & Cutlery
    Lightweight steel or bamboo utensils for convenience.
  • 🥫 Food Supplies
    Pack ready-to-cook meals, rice, pasta, or canned goods.
  • ❄️ Cooler Box
    Keep perishables like fruits, vegetables, or drinks fresh.
